SecCom 2023

30 Nov & 1 December, 2023

Prepare for the upcoming pinnacle in cybersecurity events! The esteemed Department of Cyber Science & Technology, in collaboration with the Brainware Cyber Club and the Institution Innovation Council (IIC) at Brainware University, is set to orchestrate an extraordinary program. Anticipate an illustrious celebration in honor of Computer Security Day, with an unwavering commitment to the exploration and fortification of cybersecurity. This strategic initiative transcends the ordinary event paradigm; it encapsulates a resolute mission to elevate awareness and champion the highest echelons of best practices within the dynamic landscape of cybersecurity. Envision your track poised for prominence as we assemble a conclave of luminaries, encompassing professionals from the IT and cybersecurity sectors, accomplished developers, visionary leaders, astute students, and enterprising entrepreneurs. This gathering represents not merely an event; it symbolizes a confluence of minds, a synergy of expertise under a single, grand roof. It is more than an occurrence; it is an odyssey into the future of cybersecurity—a convergence where innovation harmonizes with inspiration. Your track is positioned at the vanguard of this momentous occasion, ready to make an indelible impact on the landscape of cybersecurity.
